CRM App Schema

This interactive ERD shows the tables and relationships for the CRM Application, based on an Odoo-style lead and opportunity management system.

Tables Overview

Section titled “Tables Overview”

The schema includes the following core entities:

  • crm_lead: The central entity representing potential sales opportunities.
  • crm_team / crm_team_member: Sales organization and assignment tracking.
  • crm_stage: Pipeline stages for lead progression.
  • crm_tag: Categorization and classification of leads.
  • crm_iap_lead_ / crm_lead_scoring_**: Advanced lead mining and scoring mechanisms.
